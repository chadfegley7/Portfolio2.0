The High Flyers .Club

Tech Crunch Disrupt SF Hackathon 2016 Winner! A web based app that brings in-flight communication to the next level. You get instant access to flight data through Panasonic Flight Data API, and an IBM Watson Chat Bot that acts as your personal tour guide to accompany you on your flight. Personalized chat room so you can talk to fellow passengers on the plane so you never have to feel lonely on a flight again.


Technologies: JavaScript, MongoDB, Angular, Panasonic Flight API, IBM Watson Conversation, Mapquest API, Express.js, Socket.io, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3


Articles: Tech Crunch Article, .CLUB Article, .CLUB September Issue

Corkboard

2nd Place Hack for Homelessness A web based app that hopes to bring families together again with their loved ones that are homeless. With no login or registration, it's as easy as writing a simple email. Using Geo-Location, we are able to find your exact location and pin your virtual notes on a map using Google Maps API. Families can go on this app and search by name or location and look through our database of the homeless that have come through our app and left a note in hopes of it reaching their family. Hopefully this can be an app to help fight the homeless problem that is increasing in our communities.


Technologies: JavaScript, AJAX, MongoDB, Express.js, Geo-Locator, Google Maps API, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3

Shake Quake

Web app that allows users to visually learn in depth about earthquakes. They have access to all earthquake information from 1970 to present day including magnitude, location, date, and time. Created in 24 hours for NGA Expedition Hackathon.


Technologies: JavaScript, Angular, Node.js, Express.js, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, Mapbox, NGA Earthquake API, jQuery, Geo Locator

Flappy

An iOS based game inspired by the notorious Flappy Bird game. It has the same concept of traveling through the pipes to acquire a highscore without touching the ground or the pipes. Users also have the ability to switch the colors of the bird between Red, Green, and Blue. This is my first game developed in iOS.


Technologies: Xcode 8, SpriteKit, iOS 10, Swift 3


Expozure

An iOS based social media app based on my version of Instagram. I used a Firebase back-end that stores Users and Posts. I got more familiar with Cocoapods, bringing in FacebookSDK for Facebook Auth and Firebase. Used Keychain to keep authorized Users logged in when they close the app. Users have the ability to upload pictures from their device along with captions, then other users can then "like" posts by touching the heart icon on the post. Have a chat feature in the works right now that will be applied within the coming weeks.


Technologies: Xcode 9(Beta), iOS 11(Beta), Swift 4(Beta), Firebase, Cocoapods, Facebook Auth, Keychain
