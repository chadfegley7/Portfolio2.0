I bring uplifting, positive energy to my work environment everyday, motivating and pushing my colleagues to grow with me. "The Journey" Timeline

Cabrillo College - Santa Cruz

AUGUST 2012 - AUGUST 2014

Started attending school here to start my path towards becoming a teacher. Majoring in History at the time while trying to achieve a minor in Astronomy. Towards the end of my time at Cabrillo I started to realize that I don't have a burning desire for these studies and that I needed to be switch my path towards a subject more enticing to me.

Moved to San Francisco

AUGUST 2014

Decided to move to "The City" thinking that it would help me in my pursuit of finding my new educational path. I started working at Roy's, an upscale Hawaiian restaurant, located in the Financial District and became a bartender. Meeting the wide variety of business/tech people, I knew I was in the right place.

Found Passion For Coding

SEPTEMBER 2014

Took my first class towards a CS degree at SFCC, C#. My mind had been opened up to the tech world in a way I thought I never would have seen. I started attending coding Meet-Up's to learn from people in the business, taking in a plethora of new concepts. I had finally found what I had been looking for, my love for coding.

Moved to San Jose

AUGUST 2015

I moved to San Jose because my girlfriend was about to begin nursing school in Santa Cruz. I started looking for schools around San Jose to continue my CS Degree, that's when I found the place that would change the game for me, Coding Dojo.

Coding Dojo

Here I learned to be a Full-Stack Web Developer in 3 languages: MEAN, Python, and PHP. In 4 months I acquired the skills and knowledge to create professional web apps.
